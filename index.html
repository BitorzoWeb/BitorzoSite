Why use Bitorzo?

Explore below to see why Bitorzo is a simple, powerful, and secure Bitcoin Wallet

Instant Send and receive in just a few taps.

Your money arrives instantly in the Bitorzo wallet, which means you don’t have to pay more to get it there faster. You can also choose to keep a balance in the wallet or withdraw in your local currency when and where you want.

Easy Bitcoin Sending

Choose a contact and just send – easily send Bitcoin to your contacts with Bitorzo.

Fast & Secure

Bitorzo is fast, because it uses servers that index the Bitcoin blockchain.

No surprises. Keep more of your money

What you send is what they get. You can add, send, receive, and withdraw money from your wallet without worrying about hidden charges.

Multi-signature smart contracts.

Bitrzo payment solution leverages multi-signature transactions to enable reversible e-commerce payments, where a trusted third party – a trust agent – is nominated to resolve disputes and reverse payments in case of fraud. Using multi-signature ensures that the trust agent never has full control over the funds and cannot take them to himself. In addition, when everything goes well, the funds can be released with no intervention by the trust agent.

Free for Everyone

Bitorzo is an independent nonprofit. We're not tied to any major tech companies, and we can never be acquired by one either. Development is supported by grants and donations from people like you.
